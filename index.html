Welcome to WILD ROOTS Jungle Safari Lodge

About Us

About us Wild Roots Jungle Safari Lodge offers a peaceful escape into nature, where adventure meets comfort. Nestled in the heart of the wilderness, our lodge provides an authentic jungle experience with guided safaris, local cuisine, and warm hospitality. Whether you seek thrilling wildlife encounters or a quiet retreat surrounded by nature, Wild Roots is your perfect getaway.

Our Mission

Responsible tourism that benefits guests & community. We employ local guides, support conservation efforts, and promote sustainable travel practices while preserving Tharu heritage.
