Welcome to WILD ROOTS Jungle Safari

About Us

Wild Roots Jungle Safari & Culture Homestay is a family-run retreat in Bachhauli village, gateway to Chitwan National Park. For over 15 years, we've been sharing the magic of the jungle with authentic Tharu hospitality.

Our Mission

Responsible tourism that benefits guests & community. We employ local guides, support conservation efforts, and promote sustainable travel practices.

🧘 Yoga in the Wild

Reconnect with nature through morning and evening yoga sessions

Sunrise Yoga

Start your day with gentle asanas as the jungle awakens. Overlooking the Rapti river, our open-air deck offers peace and fresh air.

Evening Meditation

Wind down with guided meditation accompanied by the sounds of the forest – birds, cicadas, and distant calls of deer.

🔔 Sound Healing

Ancient vibrational therapy to restore balance

Himalayan Singing Bowls

Experience deep relaxation with the resonant tones of singing bowls. Sessions are held in our tranquil bamboo hut.

Forest Sound Bath

Lie down and let the gong, chimes, and nature sounds wash over you. A unique outdoor sound bath surrounded by sal trees.

🚶 Village Tour

Walk through Bachhauli and experience authentic Tharu life

Heritage Walk

Explore traditional mud houses with tribal art, meet local artisans, and learn about Tharu customs and everyday life.

Community Interaction

Share stories with village elders, watch women make handicrafts, and enjoy a cup of herbal tea in a local home.
